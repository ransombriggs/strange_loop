# Time to reimagine it

Needed to learn clojure - not ability to remember entire graph, but the ability to abstract away parts of your program, the ability to traverse abstraction.

We are no longer recipe writers, we consume and create abstractions.  And the best way to consume them is by poking at them by immediately ask questions of the abstraction.

Make a tighter connection to the software you are writing by making a stronger connection to the software you are writing.

Need to be connected to not just the software, but to the running code as well.

A difference between statically typed and dynamic runtime.
